Our LOGO

The logo’s circular outline represents student unity, seamlessly incorporating the Rod of Asclepius, the universal symbol of healing, into the name. It reflects Bibinagar scenic lake view, with the rising sun symbolizing new beginnings. The dove’s wings stand for empowerment and student rights, while the seven-petal lotus signifies knowledge and enlightenment. Infinity loops embody unity, essential for progress. The yellow-to-blue gradient blends enthusiasm, positivity, and harmony, capturing the spirit that drives the association forward
